Gentle Note

Every child develops differently, and this assessment is designed to help parents observe development with more clarity — not judgment.

There are no right or wrong answers. Every child grows at their own pace. The more honestly you respond based on everyday observations, the more meaningful the insights it shows.

Before You Begin

Take a quiet moment before answering. A parent's emotions and stress can also influence observations.

Try a simple box breathing exercise:

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Exhale for 4 seconds
  • Pause for 4 seconds

Repeat gently a few times if needed.
